Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about AIT-Bot

General Questions

What is AIT-Bot?

AIT-Bot is an AI-powered teaching assistant designed to help students and educators with various learning and teaching tasks. It can answer questions, provide explanations, and assist with educational content.

Is AIT-Bot free to use?

Yes, AIT-Bot is currently free for all users. We may introduce premium features in the future, but the core functionality will remain free for educational purposes.

Technical Support

How do I reset my password?

To reset your password, go to the login page and click on "Forgot Password." You'll receive an email with instructions to create a new password.

What browsers are supported?

AIT-Bot works best on the latest versions of Chrome, Firefox, Safari, and Edge. We recommend keeping your browser updated for the best experience.

Account Questions

How do I create an account?

Click on the "Sign Up" button on our homepage and follow the instructions. You'll need to provide a valid email address and create a password.

Can I use AIT-Bot without an account?

Yes, you can access basic features without an account. However, creating an account allows you to save your progress and access additional features.

Privacy & Security

Is my data secure with AIT-Bot?

Yes, we take data security seriously. All your information is encrypted, and we comply with strict privacy regulations to protect your data.

How can I delete my account?

You can delete your account by going to your account settings and selecting "Delete Account." This will permanently remove all your data from our systems.

Still have questions?

If you can't find what you're looking for, our support team is ready to help.
